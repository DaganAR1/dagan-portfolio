How Much Does a Website Cost for a Small Business in Denton, TX?

By Dagan May 2025 5 min read

If you've searched "how much does a website cost" recently, you've probably gotten answers ranging from $0 to $100,000 — which is about as helpful as asking how much a car costs. The honest answer is: it depends. But let's break down exactly what it depends on, so you can make a smart decision for your Denton business.

The Three Tiers of Website Pricing

1. DIY Templates (Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy) — $0–$50/month

Template builders are the cheapest entry point. You drag and drop, pick a theme, and you're live in a weekend. For a side hustle or a business that barely needs a web presence, this can be fine.

The downsides show up fast once you need to grow: slow load times (Google penalizes this), cookie-cutter designs that look like every other business, limited SEO control, and monthly fees that add up over time. After 3 years on Squarespace, you've paid $1,800+ for a site you don't own and can't move.

2. Freelance Developer — $500–$5,000

This is where most small businesses in Denton should be looking. A good freelancer builds you something custom — faster, cleaner, and actually designed around how your customers find and contact you.

Price within this range depends on scope: number of pages, whether you need booking functionality, e-commerce, custom forms, and how polished the design needs to be.

3. Agencies — $5,000–$50,000+

Agencies charge more because of overhead — account managers, project coordinators, multiple designers and developers. For a $100M company, that's worth it. For a local Denton business? You're paying for layers you don't need.

What Does a Good Website for a Denton Small Business Actually Cost?

For most local businesses — a landscaper, a salon, a contractor, a dog groomer — here's what's realistic:

My pricing (for reference)

Starter ($500 + $50/mo) — Single-page custom design, mobile-ready, contact form, basic SEO. Great for getting online fast.

Growth ($1,500 + $50/mo) — Multi-page site, advanced local SEO, booking integrations, Google reviews. Best for businesses ready to generate real leads.

The Works ($3,000 + $100/mo) — Fully custom, e-commerce, member logins, custom backend. For businesses that need their site to do heavy lifting.

The Monthly Fee Question

Most reputable developers charge a monthly retainer for hosting, updates, security patches, and support. Budget $50–$150/month for ongoing maintenance on top of your build cost.

Red Flags When Hiring a Web Developer in Denton

  • They can't show you previous work
  • They promise a site in 24 hours
  • No contract or written scope of work
  • They own the domain and hosting (you can't leave)
  • Vague pricing — "it depends on a lot of factors"

Bottom Line

For a Denton small business, a quality custom website runs $500–$3,000 upfront plus a monthly maintenance fee. That's a one-time investment that pays for itself the first time a customer finds you on Google and books a job instead of your competitor.
